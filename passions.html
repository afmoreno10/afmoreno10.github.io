Passions

Paintball

Because of the way they raised me, I have a great fascination for weapons and the military lifestyle, but I never liked the way it should be lived. Then, in 2011 I joined a paintball team that practiced a military simulation style. Since that year I have had the great pleasure of traveling to different places in Colombia, meeting new people in each place and having a lot of fun. If you ever want to go out and release some adrenaline while painting the world, I'm your man!
