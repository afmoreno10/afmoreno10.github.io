Who am I?

I am a 24 (soon to be 25) years old colombian, university student who is looking to help as many people as I am capable to reach. My main goal in life is to let people know that every problem and every situation has a wayout, we just need to keep calm, find our inner strenght and fight for who we are, who we want to be and for those who we love.

That is why my motto is "We will make everything all right" and my inner image, something like your inner animal, is a Spartan Warrior, and I am working my body and mind to become one.

My Story

Raised in a military family, the biggest learning of all is that you always need to be prepared for everything, always push your limits and always take care for your loved ones.

As I grew up, life took numerous turnings to bring me where I am, every new turn and new step brought new experiences and incredible people to learn from. Every single day has to be treated in a special way because you need to have your mind open to understand and enjoy every second of it.

Where I go?

With the broad, yet narrow approach that a universitarian life has given to me so far, one thing is taken for sure: I am not here just to watch the life past in front of me. Life has to be taken by the horns and make it our own, not one defined by the crowd. I am here to help, to raise my voice and the voice of those who need it the most so we all can fix a world that is slowly falling apart.
